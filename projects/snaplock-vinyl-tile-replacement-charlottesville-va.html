LVP Installation:

Old Flooring Removal And Replaced With LVP In Charlottesville VA

Problem: The old glue-down flooring had deteriorated, causing the planks to loosen and detach from the subfloor.

Repair: Removed the section of flooring with failing adhesive and installed new snaplock luxury vinyl plank. This required temporarily removing the base moulding and reinstalling it after the new flooring was in place.
