Interior Paint Restoration in Charlottesville VA: Trim Repair, Surface Prep, and Fine-Finish Painting:

Problem: The stairway trim and wall surfaces showed years of wear, cracked caulk and loose trim creating a rough visibly worn appearance throughout the space.

Repair: The paint finish was restored by scraping out old cracked caulk, sanding distressed surfaces, applying putty to rebuild the trim and patch the adjacent sheetrock, and installing new caulk and paint.
